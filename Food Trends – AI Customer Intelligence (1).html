Conclusion

Overall Findings

  • Overall sentiment remains positive (55%), with an average score of 0.33, indicating stable brand perception.
  • Loyal customers represent the largest segment and contribute significantly higher ratings, reinforcing strong customer retention.
  • A small but important segment of at-risk customers requires targeted engagement strategies.
  • Product-level sentiment differences highlight clear opportunities for quality and experience improvement.
  • Online channels and younger demographics drive higher engagement, suggesting strong digital growth potential.
  • Higher satisfaction levels are directly associated with increased repeat purchase behavior.
  • Consistently high-rated products show stronger long-term customer loyalty.
  • Negative feedback is concentrated around specific service and product factors, allowing focused corrective action.
  • Customer segmentation improves clarity in identifying revenue-driving groups.
  • Data-driven insights support more structured planning and operational improvements.
